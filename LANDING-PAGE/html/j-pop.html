Indigo la End is a Japanese rock band formed in 2010, led by vocalist, guitarist, and songwriter Enon Kawatani. Known for their melodic, emotional indie-rock sound, they are associated with acts like Gesu no Kiwami Otome. The band consists of Enon Kawatani, Osada Curtis (guitar), Gochou Ryousuke (bass), and Sato Eitaro (drums).
