Violet Evergarden

Violet Evergarden, a former child soldier adjusting to life after the war, becomes an Auto Memory Doll — a professional writer who helps others express their feelings in beautiful letters. Through her journey recording emotions she didn’t understand before, Violet learns the meaning of love, loss, and what truly connects people.

Director: Taichi Ishidate

Main Cast (Japanese): Yui Ishikawa (Violet Evergarden), Erika Kohama, Mutsumi Tamura, Takehito Koyasu
