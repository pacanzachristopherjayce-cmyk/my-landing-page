Sira na ang utak ko dahil sa instagram reels

Christopher Jayce Pacanza
January 16, 2026

One more reel

"Isapang reel! Maybe the next reel is going to be more funny or interesting!" For those who use instagram reels like me everyday we experience this feeling every time we use it, parang it feels like naka "glue" yung mata sa screen ng phone mo. Minsan hindi mo namamalayan na ilang minuto na pala ang lumipas, kasi sobrang dali lang mag swipe pataas. Parang automatic na yung kamay mo kahit wala ka namang specific na hinahanap. You tell yourself na saglit lang, pampalipas oras lang, pero bago mo mapansin naka ilang reels ka na pala. It becomes part of your daily routine without you even realizing it, without you even realizing it.

Ok pa ba ako?

For those who use instagram reels everyday for more than 1 hr, tingin nyo ba, ok pa ba kayo? In my own experience instagram reels is very addicting, most of the time pag na nonood ako ng reels, 2 hrs feels like 5 mins, time flies by really fast. Not only that ig reels is addicting, it also ruined my attention span so bad that I can't focus pag nag lelecture yung mga professors ko. It also destroys our mental health, with content changing every three to thiry seconds, you experience sudden change in mood, if you use it like lets say for 5 hours, after that ma de deppress ka, mapapatanong ka nalang sa sarili mo na bakit ka bigla na depress. Napapansin ko din na mas nagiging impatient ako sa mga bagay na mabagal, like reading books or watching long educational videos. Parang nasanay na yung utak ko sa mabilisang entertainment. Kahit gusto kong mag focus sa school works ko, may urge pa rin na i-check yung phone ko. It slowly affects productivity, sleep schedule, and even motivation to do important tasks. Sometimes you feel guilty after using it for too long, but the next day you still end up doing the same thing again, doing the same thing again.

Balance is the key

So far, what I really think na kailangan ko gawin is to balance using it every day. Like for example, limiting to only using it for 30 mins everyday. Yes this is hard, especially if this is your first time limiting yourself using ig reels, but always asks yourself, ok pa ba ako? Maybe setting small rules for myself like not using it before studying or before sleeping can help. It does not mean completely removing it from my life, but learning how to control it. At the end of the day, technology should help us and not control us. If we are aware of our habits and slowly adjust them, maybe we can enjoy reels without sacrificing our focus, mental health, and personal growth.
